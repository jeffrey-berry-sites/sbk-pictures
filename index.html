About

Jeffrey Berry is an award-winning film director and writer with over 30 years of creative achievement and business success in the production of TV commercials, documentaries, corporate films, interactive media, web content and 70 mm giant screen IMAX films.

He is a co-founder of SBK Pictures, a force in the television commercial business for more than three decades. Jeffrey has directed national commercials for Coke, Pepsi, Sprint, Campbellâ€™s, Kraft, Merrill Lynch, Mattel, Pillsbury, Merck, Pfizer and many more. He has written, directed and/or developed 7 IMAX films, including feature documentary films for the Olympics and for Disney.

Jeffrey is a co-founder of Medical Broadcasting Company, now Digitas Health. MBC was an early leader in creating commercials and video content for the pharmaceutical and health sciences industry. Digitas Health is a global leader in digital marketing for health brands.

He is a co-founder of eMoney Advisor, a leader in providing digital wealth management solutions for financial advisors and their high net worth clients. Jeffrey served as Executive Creative Director and was writer/director of a large library of award winning animated videos called Advisor Branded Media. Jeffrey directed celebrity performers Donald Sutherland, Sir Ian McKellen, Tom Selleck, Diane Lane, Kiefer Sutherland and Rob Lowe.

Jeffrey is as experienced in the boardroom strategizing campaigns with CEOs, as he is directing video crews on location, or collaborating with editors and composers in the studio.
